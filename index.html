What is EVE?

▾
EVE Glove is an advanced wearable that turns hand gestures into precise digital input — designed for immersive control and haptic feedback.

How to install?

▾
Download the Windows app from our download page, follow the installer steps, and pair the glove via Bluetooth or USB.

Is it ergonomic?

▾
Yes — EVE Glove is designed with ergonomic materials and adjustable straps to minimize fatigue.

Is it wireless?

▾
Yes, EVE supports wireless connectivity and offers a wired fallback for low-latency use cases.
