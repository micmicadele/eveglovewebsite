How to Use EVE Glove

Finger Tracking

The glove detects finger movements for mouse click actions. Bend your index finger to perform a left-click and your middle finger for a right-click.

Cursor Control

Move the mouse cursor using your hand tilts. Gently tilt your hand in the desired direction to guide the cursor smoothly across the screen.
